The NAMU Mission

NAMU is the Korean word for tree. The tree is a symbolic icon that represents steady growth in all weather conditions. The shield represents protection from external risks. NAMU Capital is focused on providing greater access to private equity investment opportunities to all investors. NAMU achieves this through crowdfunding and leveraging the Tokenization technology to provide transparency and simplifying the transfer of ownership. NAMU is also committed to strategically place investments in Impact assets aimed at being environmentally conscious and developing marginalized communities.
